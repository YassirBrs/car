    Famous places in Agadir and the surf coast

    • Kasbah Oufella

      Historic hilltop ruins with one of the best panoramic views over Agadir and the bay.

      Best for sunset views

    • Souk El Had

      Agadir's biggest market for spices, souvenirs, fresh produce, and local everyday life.

      Great for shopping

    • Agadir Marina

      A clean waterfront promenade with restaurants, cafes, and relaxed evening walks by the harbor.

      Best for dining

    • Agadir Corniche

      The beachfront walkway where you can enjoy ocean views, cafes, and a laid-back seaside atmosphere.

      Best for beach walks

    • Crocoparc Agadir

      A family-friendly park with gardens, exotic plants, and crocodiles, just outside the city center.

      Great for families

    • Taghazout

      A relaxed surf village north of Agadir with beaches, cafes, and a laid-back coastal vibe.

      Best for surf stays

    • Anza

      Known for its surf breaks, long beach walks, and easy access from the center of Agadir.

      Best for beach breaks

    • Imsouane

      A legendary surf bay for long rides, relaxed fishing-town energy, and beautiful Atlantic views.

      Best for long waves

    • Surf coast day trips

      Use your rental car to hop between surf beaches, hidden viewpoints, and seaside cafes.

      Best for road trips
